Ramen

Ingredients: Noodles, broth, sliced pork, green onions, soft-boiled egg, miso paste.

Procedure:

Heat the broth in a pot, then add soy sauce and miso paste.

Cook the ramen noodles according to package instructions.

Divide the noodles into bowls and pour the hot broth over them.

Top with sliced pork, boiled eggs, and green onions.

Sushi

Ingredients: Vinegar, rice, fresh fish, nori, soy sauce, , sugar, wasabi.

Procedure:

Rinse the rice, then cook it with water.

Mix rice vinegar and sugar, then combine with the cooked rice.

Shape the rice into small balls and top with fresh fish.

Wrap with nori if desired.

Tonkatsu

Ingredients: 200g thinly sliced beef 80ml tsuyu concentrated soup stock 1 onion 380g cooked rice shichimi chilli powder benishoga pickled red ginger.

Procedure:

Start by cutting up a whole onion into thin slices and slicing the beef very finely.

Add 80ml of tsuyu and 80ml of water to a frying pan and turn on the heat.

After 5 minutes, add 1 onion (sliced) to the pan and allow it to cook for a minute or two, or until the onions are soft.

Garnish the dish with beni shoga pickled red ginger and shichimi chilli powder before serving.
